Stage Detail

Who creates the deal

  • SDR does the initial call — but does NOT qualify
  • AE (or Sasu) does the actual qualification and creates the deal
  • Deal auto-created at SQL, or AE creates manually after qualifying the call
  • Key lead fields should copy across (pain, source, compelling event)

Seminal Questions (Three Whys)

  • Why do anything? — pain in status quo must be documented
  • Why now? — compelling event within 12 months confirmed
  • Why Podero? — alignment with key business initiative

Minimum Entry Bar (enforced)

  • ICP confirmed (company + contact fit)
  • Pain in status quo documented (text field — mandatory)
  • Compelling event exists (yes/no — mandatory)
  • Path to decision makers confirmed
  • 2/5 SUGAR minimum met
